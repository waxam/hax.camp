  1. Attend a HAX camp
  2. Star, Fork, Clone the HAXcms repo
  3. Install HAX or HAXcms either via HAXonDemand , ReclaimHosting or setup your own (we support ddev, drupal 7/8, backdrop, classicpress, wordpress, gravcms, docker and W/MAMP installs as it's a flat file system that only requires apache2.4 / php 7.1+)
  4. Tweet / FB / insta / whatev a photo of the hax camp poster with #HAXTheWeb #webcomponents and @haxcamp
  5. Send out a photo of a hockey jersey (there will be many) with #HAXTheWeb #webcomponents and @haxcamp
  6. Live blog sessions on HAXcms that you attend making sure to use the same hash tags for people to follow along and send @haxcamp  a link to your blog
  7. Let us send out a photo of you in your sweet threads and take a "team photo" when we "fill out the roster"
  8. Walk away with a sick jersey to keep and spread the good vibes of team HAXTheWeb!
